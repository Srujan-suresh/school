COURSE DETAILS

Education from Pre-KG to Class 10

CES Public School offers quality education from Pre-KG to Class 10, following the Karnataka State Board syllabus. Our academic structure supports students at every stage of their learning journey.

With experienced teachers and a disciplined learning environment, the school focuses on strong fundamentals, concept clarity, and overall personality development.

  • Pre-Primary: Pre-KG, LKG & UKG
  • Primary School: Classes 1 to 5
  • Upper Primary: Classes 6 to 8
  • High School: Classes 9 & 10 (SSLC)
  • Karnataka State Board syllabus
  • English medium instruction

Academic Programs & Curriculum

CES Public School follows the Karnataka State Board syllabus and offers structured education from Pre-KG to Class 10, focusing on academic excellence and holistic development.

FOUNDATION STAGE

Pre-KG to Class 5

Early education at CES Public School focuses on building a strong foundation through activity-based and interactive learning. Children are encouraged to explore, question, and develop essential skills.

  • Karnataka State Board syllabus
  • Activity-based and child-friendly learning
  • Strong focus on literacy & numeracy
  • Overall growth through academics & activities
MIDDLE SCHOOL

Classes 6 to 8

The middle school curriculum strengthens conceptual understanding and encourages independent thinking. Students are guided to develop analytical and problem-solving abilities.

  • Karnataka State Board curriculum
  • Concept-oriented teaching approach
  • Continuous assessments and guidance
  • Balanced academic and personal development
HIGH SCHOOL

Classes 9 & 10

High school education at CES Public School is designed to prepare students for the SSLC examinations with focused learning, regular assessments, and personal academic mentoring.

  • Karnataka State Board (SSLC)
  • Exam-oriented and structured teaching
  • Regular tests, revisions & feedback
  • Guidance for academic excellence

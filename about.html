About Us

Welcome to CES Public School

CES Public School is committed to nurturing young minds through quality education, strong values, and holistic development. Located on Hesaraghatta Road, our school provides a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment where children are encouraged to explore their potential.

With experienced faculty, modern teaching methods, and a balanced focus on academics, sports, and co-curricular activities, we ensure every child grows with confidence, discipline, and curiosity.

  • Experienced & caring faculty
  • Strong academic foundation
  • Focus on sports & extracurricular activities
  • Safe and student-friendly campus

Our Mission & Vision

At CES Public School, our mission and vision focus on nurturing young minds through quality education, strong values, and holistic development. We strive to create a supportive and inspiring environment where students grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a strong educational foundation that encourages curiosity, creativity, discipline, and confidence, enabling students to achieve academic excellence and become responsible citizens.

At CES Public School, we aim to:

  • Deliver quality education with modern teaching methods.
  • Encourage critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Promote moral values, discipline, and respect.
  • Support overall personality development of every child.
  • Create a safe, caring, and inclusive learning environment.
  • Prepare students for academic and life challenges.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading school that inspires lifelong learning, innovation, and excellence, shaping confident individuals who contribute positively to society.

We envision a future where students:

  • Excel academically with strong foundational skills.
  • Develop leadership, teamwork, and communication abilities.
  • Embrace creativity, innovation, and technology.
  • Grow with confidence, integrity, and responsibility.
  • Adapt to a changing world with resilience and optimism.
  • Become responsible global citizens with strong values.

Annual Day Celebration

Our Annual Day is a showcase of talent, creativity, and confidence. Students perform cultural programs, skits, and musical performances, making it a vibrant celebration of the academic year. Parents and teachers enjoy the festivities, applauding the efforts and achievements of our young stars.

Sports Day

Sports Day promotes fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition. Students participate in races, relay events, and team games, demonstrating sportsmanship and determination. It’s a fun and energetic day that encourages children to stay active and confident.

Children's Day Celebration

On Children’s Day, the school is filled with laughter and joy. Fun games, activities, and interactive sessions make the day special for our students. Teachers organize surprises and treats, ensuring every child feels celebrated and appreciated.
